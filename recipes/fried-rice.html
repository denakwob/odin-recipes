Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice is a combination of long grained rice, mixture of warm peas, carrots and onions with scrambled eggs mixed all together! You will not be getting take out any longer!

  • Cooked rice
  • Vegetables- Peas and Carrots
  • Eggs
  • Small white Onion
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Green Onions
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat Skillet and pour some sesame oil into the bottom of the skillet
  2. Chop the onion and carrots, with the peas add all to the oil and fry until it is tender
  3. Push the veggies to the side of the skillet
  4. Add your mixed eggs on the otherside of the veggies
  5. Use a spatula to scramble the eggs and mix with the veggies
  6. Add the rice and stir with to mix with the eggs and vegetables
  7. Pour the soy sauce on top and stir until it is mixed thoroughly and the color changes to somewhat light gold
  8. Garnish with green onions and serve with either chicken, sausages or beef.
