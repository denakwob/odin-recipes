Jollof

Description

Jollof is prepared by cooking rice in a rich tomato sauce with spices and stock, with all the flavours
melded together to create a delightful eating experience.

It can be cooked with meat, fish and vegetables; and served at festivals, parties and get-togethers

Ingredients

  • 2 Red bell peppers
  • 5 Tomatoes
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1/2 cup Sunflower oil
  • Tomato paste/puree
  • Curry powder
  • Dried thyme
  • Bay leaves
  • Chicken stock
  • Chicken stock cubes
  • Rice
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the tomatoes and peppper and peel the onions
  2. Wash the rice under cold water and drain the water using a colander
  3. Cut a quarter of the onion into pieces and drop them in a blender
    in a ddition with three tomatoes, red pepper and the scotch bonnet pepper.
  4. Add a cup of water to it and blend until the content is liquified
  5. Now pick the remaining onion and slice them
  6. Slice the remaining tomatoes thinly
  7. Now place a pan of reasonable size on fire and pour reasonable
    amount of oil in the pan to be heated
  8. Add the chopped onions and stire until they look start to look like caramel
  9. Add the tomato paste and reduce heat. Use wooden laddle to stir until it fries for 5 mins
  10. Raise the heat to medium and pour twoo cups of the blended peppers mix into the pan.
  11. Add the curry powder, nutmeg, and bay leaves, then sprinkle in the stock cubes
  12. Mix well with a wooden spoon and allow to cook while stirring occasionally
  13. Cook until the moisture has reduced significantly and the oil
    has settled on the surface. This will take at least 20 mins.
  14. Add the chicken stock and mix the rice into the pan. Then add 1 cup of water, taste
    for salt and add more if needed
  15. Allow the rice to come to a boil, then reduce the heat.
  16. Cover the pan with a clean cloth .
  17. Check after 10 mins and stir the rice with a wooden spoon
  18. Repeat more frequently the until the rice is cooked through
    and has absorbed all the sauce. This will take at least 30 mins.
  19. Remove from heat, stir with a wooden spoon, put the lid back on and
    leave for a few minutes before serving.
  20. Serve with toppings, salad, coleslaw, shito ,and chicken
